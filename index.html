Video Games Through Time

1970-1979

Beginnings

The first arcade and home console games laid the foundation of the industry.

1972 Pong - First commercial video game
1977 Atari 2600 - First mass‑market success
1978 Space Invaders - Revolutionizes arcades

1980-1989

Golden Era

The boom of arcade games and the rise of the first modern consoles.

1980 Pac-Man - Pop culture icon
1983 Nintendo Entertainment System - Revives the industry
1985 Super Mario Bros - Defines platformers

1990-1999

The 3D Revolution

The era of 3D graphics and PC gaming transforms the industry.

1993 Doom - Pioneer of 3D FPS
1994 PlayStation - Revolutionizes gaming
1996 Super Mario 64 - Sets 3D standards

2000-2009

Online Era

Online gaming and HD graphics dominate the decade.

2003 Call of Duty - Defines modern shooters
2004 World of Warcraft - Revolutionizes MMOs
2005 Xbox 360 - Pushes modern online gaming

2010-Prezent

Modern Era

AAA games, virtual reality, and cloud gaming define the present.

2017 Fortnite - Transforms social gaming
2020 PlayStation 5 - Next‑gen gaming
2022 Elden Ring - Redefines RPGs
