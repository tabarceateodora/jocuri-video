Gaming Glossary

A mini dictionary with common gaming terms. Hover over each term for a subtle animation and highlight.

Basic Terms

FPS
First-Person Shooter – action seen through the character's eyes; focus on shooting.
RPG
Role-Playing Game – you play a character, progress via quests, skills, and story.
MMO
Massively Multiplayer Online – online games with many players on the same server.
MOBA
Multiplayer Online Battle Arena – two teams fight on a symmetric map for strategic objectives.
RTS
Real-Time Strategy – real-time strategy, managing resources, bases, and armies.
Sandbox
Open-world games with high freedom to explore and create (e.g., Minecraft).
Roguelike
Procedurally generated levels, high difficulty, progress lost on death (permadeath).
Hitbox
Invisible area of an object used for collisions and hits.
Cooldown
Cooldown time before an ability can be used again.
XP
Experience – points earned to progress or level up.
